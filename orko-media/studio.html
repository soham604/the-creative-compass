The story

A $100,000 investment to build the studio Montréal deserved.

Orko Media is the story of Soham Dey — an entrepreneur who took a risk at 17 by dropping out of college to buy a camera, without even knowing how to use it.

A few years later, that bet became a professional studio in the heart of Montréal. After months of hard work and a $100,000 investment, the Orko studio now offers 12 unique sets — from clean minimalist to cinematic luxury.

All professional equipment is included: camera, lighting, mic and teleprompter. Whether it's a podcast, webinar, social content or high-end production — you arrive, you set up, you shoot. No compromise.

FAQ

Common questions.

Where is the studio located?

The studio is at 111 Rue Chabanel O, Suite 410, Montréal, QC H2N 1C3. On-site parking is available for crew and clients.

What's included in every booking?

Everything is included: professional camera, studio lighting, professional microphone, teleprompter, work desk, internet connection and access to all 12 sets. Just arrive with your idea.

How many sets are available?

12 unique sets, each with its own atmosphere — from clean minimalist to cinematic luxury, multi-camera podcast setups and tropical environments.

Can I record a podcast at the studio?

Absolutely. Several sets are specifically designed for podcasts, with professional microphones, multi-camera setups and custom ambiance. Forum, Oasis and Legacy are particularly popular for podcast recordings.

Can I bring my own crew and equipment?

Yes — external crews are welcome. You can use our included equipment package or bring your own. Let us know in advance and we'll prepare the studio to your spec.

Do you offer post-production as well?

Yes. We offer a full post-production service — editing, colour grading, sound design and delivery. Get in touch and we'll put together a package that covers everything.
