Common questions

FAQ

How long does a typical video project take?

Most brand film projects run 4–8 weeks from brief to delivery. Shorter social content can turn around in 1–2 weeks. We'll give a specific timeline at quote stage.

Do you work with clients outside the UK?

Absolutely. We work with international clients regularly and have produced projects across Europe, North America, and Asia. Remote collaboration is seamless for creative and post work.

What's included in your day rates?

Our day rate quotes include the core crew, standard camera package, and basic lighting. Additional crew, specialist kit, locations, talent, and travel are itemised separately so everything is transparent.

How many revision rounds are included?

Video projects include two rounds of revisions as standard. Photography retouching includes one round. Additional rounds can be scoped and quoted if needed.
