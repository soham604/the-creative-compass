History

Our journey.

2017

Founded in a warehouse

Alex Orko and Maya Rahman launch Orko Media from a converted East London warehouse with two cameras and a single edit suite.

2019

First major brand campaign

Orko produces a national TV campaign for a FTSE 100 company — establishing the studio as a serious commercial production house.

2021

Studio expansion

We move into our current 4,000 sq ft purpose-built studio with a full cyclorama wall, professional sound booth, and dedicated colour suite.

2023

Industry recognition

Orko wins Best Production Company at the British Film Craft Awards, with three further nominations for client work across Europe.

2025

Today

18 full-time creatives, 200+ projects delivered, clients across technology, fashion, automotive, and more.
